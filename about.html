Our Story

Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts.

  • A Chance Encounter

    Summer 2017, San Antonio

    Our story begins in the summer of 2017, in the heart of San Antonio, TX. Fresh from completing our first year of medical school, we found ourselves enrolled in a 6-week-long Basic Officer Leadership Course. Fate (and the alphabet) put us in the same group Shah and Smolinsky were destined to meet. What began as a simple afternoon in the library blossomed into an instant friendship, strengthened by shared training, workouts, meals, and Todd’s unofficial role as Sheni’s personal ice cream chauffeur. The bond deepened at Camp Bullis, where we paired up for land navigation. Todd insisted on his sense of direction, only to learn (as he would many times after) that Sheni was right. We left San Antonio that summer as part of an unforgettable friend group unaware of what was beginning to grow between us.

  • From Friendship to Something More

    2018–2019

    In the months and years that followed, we kept in touch through group chats and dinner reunions in NYC. But in the fall of 2018, something shifted we began talking more often, more intentionally. One visit turned into many, and a spark turned into something undeniably special. Whether it was a Philly night out, a holiday trip, or a carefully coordinated interview rotation in cities like Tacoma, San Antonio, and Honolulu we prioritized time with each other. And in December 2019, life gave us the biggest gift: we matched into residency programs together in San Antonio. Sheni in OBGYN, Todd in General Surgery. The stage was set.

  • Building a Life

    2020–2022

    In the summer of 2020, long-distance became a thing of the past. We moved in together and began the most intense chapters of our careers. Residency tested us in every way, but it also taught us what resilience, love, and true partnership really mean. We celebrated our milestones buying our first home in spring 2021, adopting our sweet dog Trey that fall, and traveling to new places whenever we could squeeze it in. Through every high and low, we showed up for each other. And in 2022, love only grew deeper, leading us to a December trip we’ll never forget.

  • The Proposal

    December 30, 2022

    Washington D.C. during the holidays was magical. Surrounded by our closest friends, it felt like the perfect place for the next chapter to begin. Todd tried to play it cool, but Sheni’s suspicions rose quickly especially during their stroll through the botanical gardens. Even amid the holiday crowds, everything else faded when Todd got down on one knee by the Christmas tree at the water gardens. It was just the two of us. And just like that, surrounded by twinkling lights, laughter, and love we got engaged.
